What is in the summary view?

The summary view contains:

  • address and title number
  • owner name and address details
  • any lender name and address details
  • tenure, title quality and price paid details
  • an indication of the location and general boundaries
What are title documents?

Title documents are available in PDF format to download and keep.

The title register has details about the property or land, including:

  • title number
  • who owns it
  • what they paid for it (properties only, if available)
  • any rights of way
  • whether a mortgage on it has been ‘discharged’, eg paid off

The title plan includes details of:

  • the property’s location
  • the general boundaries
