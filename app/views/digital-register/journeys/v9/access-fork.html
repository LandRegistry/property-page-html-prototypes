What is in the summary view?

The summary view of the title contains:

  • Address and title number
  • Owner name and address details
  • Lender name and address details
  • Tenure, quality and price paid details
  • An indication of the location and size of the land
What are full title documents?

Full title documents are available in PDF format to download and keep. They are a full copy of the title held by the government, containing:
